About ShriTech Travel

Learn more about our travel agency and what we offer

Welcome to ShriTech Travel, your gateway to exploring the incredible beauty of Maharashtra and beyond! Based in Kalyan, we are passionate about crafting unique and unforgettable travel experiences. We understand that every journey is personal, which makes us special because we specialize in creating customized travel packages tailored to your specific desires, whether you're seeking a serene escape, a thrilling adventure, or a perfect family holiday.

From the misty hills of Lonavala and Khandala, the serene charm of Matheran, to the breathtaking landscapes of Mahabaleshwar & Panchgani, and the coastal tranquility of Alibaug & Kashid – we've got Maharashtra covered. But our horizons extend further; we also design personalized tours across diverse Indian destinations like Goa, Kerala, Himachal, and Uttarakhand, ensuring you discover the true essence of each place.

At ShriTech Travel, we believe that travel should be seamless and stress-free. Our dedicated team of travel experts takes care of every detail, from meticulously planning your itinerary to arranging comfortable transport and providing round-the-clock support. We pride ourselves on offering reliable, affordable, and personalized services that guarantee a smile on your face and cherished memories that last a lifetime. Let us turn your travel dreams into a beautiful reality!

Our Promise: Safe, affordable, and personalized travel services with a smile!
